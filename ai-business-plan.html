Example Prompts

Sample Prompt Chains for Each Phase

Get started with these example prompts designed to guide your business planning process.

Phase 1: Discovery Prompts

1. Describe the core problem your AI solution addresses
2. List 3-5 potential customer segments
3. What existing solutions are customers using today?
4. Why is now the right time for your solution?
5. What metrics would validate product-market fit?

Phase 2: Architecture Prompts

1. What AI models/frameworks best fit your use case?
2. Describe your data collection and processing pipeline
3. How will you handle model training and updates?
4. What infrastructure will support your solution?
5. How will you monitor and maintain quality?

Phase 3: Execution Prompts

1. What is your pricing strategy and why?
2. List your customer acquisition channels
3. What are your key costs and revenue drivers?
4. Describe your 12-month launch roadmap
5. How will you measure and optimize performance?
